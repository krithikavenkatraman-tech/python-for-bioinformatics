# DNA to RNA Transcription

## Objective

Convert a DNA sequence into RNA.

---

## Biological Background

During transcription:

DNA

↓

Messenger RNA (mRNA)

Thymine (T) is replaced by Uracil (U).

---

## Example

DNA

```
ATGCGT
```

RNA

```
AUGCGU
```

---
